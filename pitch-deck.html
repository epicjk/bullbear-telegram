üíµ Business Model

Revenue is generated through the house edge ‚Äî the difference between the theoretical fair payout (2.0x for 50/50 odds) and the actual payout (1.95x base).

üíé Revenue Streams
  • Direct Betting Edge: 2.5% (1.95x payout on 50/50 odds)
  • AI Bot Fees: 1.5% - 10% based on performance tier
  • Estimated Blended Edge: ~3.4% across all betting activity
  • Volume-Based Model: Profit scales linearly with transaction volume

The dynamic fee structure incentivizes bot competition ‚Äî bots that perform well attract more followers but also charge higher fees, balancing the ecosystem.
